Welcome to Git-Help humans,

We are a group of people who agree that difficulties must be overcome, and the services that help people with visual impairment be provided in order to be equal with people and be able to accomplish what they want without obstacles.

services provided:

Voice Recording, and audio library

The person with visual impairment requests a voice recording service, or searches in the audio library for what he wants. The audio library contains a huge collection of books and audio novels

Reading

A person with visual impairment requests a person to serve face-to-face reading and selects the language in which he wants to read.

Print and convert files

The person with a visual impairment requests to print or convert files from format to format

Technical support

The person with visual impairment requests technical support service by communicating with Git-Help via E-mail or via direct contact. In the technical support service, we repair talking devices or configure computers for people with visual impairments and download appropriate programs for them.
